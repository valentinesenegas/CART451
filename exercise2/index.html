The Fables of La Fontaine

Jean de La Fontaine was a French author. He wrote the "Fables" of La Fontaine, which are very well known stories that always have a lesson at the end.

The texts that I have chosen are The Council held by the rats, The Dog and Cat, The Fly and The Game, The Frog That Wished to be as Big As The Ox, The Grasshopper and the Ant, The Raven and the Fox, The wo Bulls and the Frog, The Wolf Accusing The Fox Before The Monkey.

Number of characters in bigrams

With the use of NGrams, I was able to know more about bigrams and trigrams.

We see that there are no bigrams with less than 3 characters, which is normal. We see that the majority of bigrams are made out of 7 to 11 characters. This seems to correlate with the number of letters per word, and a bigram is made of two words.

Number of characters in Trigrams

We see that there are no bigrams with less than 7 characters, which makes sense. To make a trigram, 3 words are needed, and it makes sense that these words contain at least 2 characters. We see that the majority of trigrams are made out of 7 to 18 characters. This seems to correlate with the number of letters per word, and a trigram is made of 3 words.
